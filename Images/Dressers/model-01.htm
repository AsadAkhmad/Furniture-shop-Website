      Key Features

      • Solid wood legs
      • Mid-century, minimalist style
      • High-density foam filled cushions
      • Swyft's locking system for quick, easy installation without the need for tools.
      • Available in 30 fabrics
      Perfect for those who might be limited on space, the Model 02 2-seater sofa is compact without compromising on style. The mid-century design boasts clean, simple lines and high-density foam base cushions for optimum comfort.

      Dimensions

      • Width 175cm
      • Height 82cm
      • Depth 85cm
      • Seat Height: 43cm
      • Leg Height: 18.5cm
      • No of boxes: 2

      Fabric composition

      • Velvet: 100% Polyester
      • Linen: 73% Polyester, 27% Cotton
      • Faux Leather: 91% Polyester 9% cotton
      • Boucle: 96% Polyester, 4% Acrylic

      • Chenille: 100% Polyester
      • Leather: 100% Real Leather
